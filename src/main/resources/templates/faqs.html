💳 Membership & Payments

Premium members enjoy a range of exclusive benefits including: viewing full contact details of matches, unlimited messaging, priority profile visibility in search results, advanced search filters, the ability to see who viewed your profile, and access to dedicated customer support. Premium membership significantly increases your chances of finding a compatible match.
We offer several membership plans to suit your needs: Free (basic profile creation and limited browsing), Silver (3-month plan with contact viewing and messaging), Gold (6-month plan with all Silver benefits plus priority listing), and Platinum (12-month plan with all features plus dedicated relationship manager). Visit our Membership page for current pricing details.
We accept multiple payment methods including Credit/Debit Cards, Net Banking, UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), and popular digital wallets. All transactions are secured with SSL encryption. To purchase, go to Membership > Choose Plan > Pay Now and select your preferred payment method.
Occasionally, there may be a delay in processing due to bank or network issues. Please wait up to 30 minutes and refresh your account. If your membership is still not active, please contact our support team at contact@godswillmatrimony.in with your transaction ID and payment screenshot. We'll resolve it promptly.
We regularly run seasonal offers and promotional discounts on our membership plans. To stay updated, subscribe to our newsletter or check the Offers section on the website. You can also follow our social media pages for exclusive promo codes and limited-time deals.
