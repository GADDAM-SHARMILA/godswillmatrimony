Privacy Policy

Data Protection Guarantee

All data is 100% secure and safe. Even the God's Will Matrimony team cannot view your sensitive private information.

Your data is encrypted and protected using 256-bit industrial-grade protection. This ensures that your personal details remain unreadable to any unauthorized entities.

We use cookies to improve your browsing experience, remember your login state, and provide personalized partner recommendations.

Your personal data is retained only as long as your account remains active. Upon account deletion, all associated data is permanently and irreversibly removed from our servers within 30 days.

We do not share, sell, or disclose your personal information to any third parties without your explicit consent, except where required by law or necessary to provide our core services.

6. Profile Visibility

Your profile is only visible to registered and verified members of the platform. You can control your profile visibility settings from your account dashboard at any time.

7. Communication Privacy

All messages exchanged between members are end-to-end encrypted. Our team does not monitor or read private conversations unless flagged for safety review by a member.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. To exercise these rights, please contact our support team through the platform's official support channel.

9. Security Measures

We implement strict technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Important Security Reminder

  • Never share your password or OTP with anyone, including our team.
